About Us

FarmTalo is a Somali-based agricultural advisory platform founded by passionate agricultural professionals. Our goal is to empower farmers with the right knowledge, tools, and support to improve their productivity, sustainability, and income. We believe that smart farming starts with accessible and reliable information.

Our Services

✔️ Soil testing and fertility recommendations
✔️ Crop rotation planning and pest management
✔️ Livestock feeding and health advisory
✔️ Modern irrigation and water-saving techniques
✔️ Online training and consultation for farmers
